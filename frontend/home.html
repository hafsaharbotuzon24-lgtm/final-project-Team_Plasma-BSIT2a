Combat Coders

Enter a world of fast-paced 2D combat where combat meets coding. Choose your role, learn the ways of coding, and fight your way to victory against evil bugs that dare to initiate a fiersome duel!

Play Your Own Way

Combat Coders is an interactive, game-based learning system designed to improve players' coding skills while enhancing strategic and decision-making abilities. Players navigate using a card-driven system (similar to tarot cards), choosing paths that lead to treasure rooms, battles, or special events. At the start, players choose between the Knight, Witch, or Rogue (purely cosmetic), then progress by solving coding challenges to advance through increasingly difficult levels.

Brain-tangling battles!

When a player chooses the "battle" path, a bug appears and presents coding questions involving HTML, CSS, or JavaScript. The player must answer correctly to deal damage. Right solutions hurt the enemy, while wrong answers reduce the player's health. After each battle, the player proceeds and is given new choices: chest, battle, or room.

Learn Through Interactive Tutorial

Get hands-on with the core mechanics through a guided interactive tutorial. Learn the fundamentals step by step, so you are ready to compete from your very first match.

Tough Bosses

The boss fight begins when the player reaches the final path of a certain level. The boss provides a code snippet containing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with blanks that the user must fill in or answer correctly. Defeating the boss allows the player to proceed to the next level.
