Game Mechanics

-- Choosing between cards --

1. Battle - A code to solve.

2. Room - Will provide HP

3. Chest - Will provide hints

-- Battle --

If you win and still have maximum hearts, you will receive both a heart and a hint.

-- Room --

A room will appear and provide you a heart.

-- Chest --

A chest will appear and provide you with a hint. *Non-stackable*

-- Boss --

After clearing a certain number of paths, you will reach the final task, the Boss room.

-- Battle --

An insect monster will appear and give you a challenge
